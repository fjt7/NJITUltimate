About Us:

Founded in 1894, in a small fishing village off the coast of Tuscany, there lived a man by the name of Lasagna. He prophecied that one day a baby would be born, who would be the "chosen one" and rule of the land. That baby grew up to be the man we all know as Coach Jason, and thus the prophecy was fulfilled and the NJIT ultimate frisbee team was formed.
